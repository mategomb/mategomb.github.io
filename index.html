About

My name is Máté Gombkötő, and I'm a front-end developer currently living and working in Dortmund, Germany.

I started off professionally on the front end of the web about two years ago and I'm one of those crazy few, who loves writing vanilla CSS without any library. But of course I'm no stranger to SASS/SCSS and Tailwind either.

I have worked with JavaScript and I'm also familiar with Vue.js when it comes to building web applications. I'm also learning how to use React and Redux with TypeScript at the moment in my spare time.

In the future, I would love to have more opportunity to use React, Tailwind CSS, and even Node.js on the backend professionally.

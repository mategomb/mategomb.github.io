About

My name is Máté Gombkötő, and I'm a front-end developer living and working in Dortmund, Germany.

I started off professionally on the front end of the web three years ago and I'm one of those crazy few, who loves writing vanilla CSS without any component/UI library. But of course I'm no stranger to SASS/SCSS and Tailwind either.

I have worked with JavaScript/TypeScript with and without a framework, and I'm also familiar with React and Vue.js when it comes to building web applications.

In the future, I would love to have more opportunity to work on the backend, on SQL-databases, and perhaps cross-platform mobile applications as well.
